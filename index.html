Neha Karanjkar

 





Assistant Professor,
Computer Science and Engineering Department
Indian Institute of Technology Goa (IIT Goa)



Welcome to my webpage. I am an assistant professor in the CSE department at IIT Goa.
I received my M.Tech and Ph.D degrees from IIT Bombay in 2017 and was a post-doc at
the Robert Bosch Centre for Cyber-Physical Systems, IISc Bangalore between 2017-2018.

My research interests are in modeling, simulation and optimization of discrete-event systems
in the application areas of Internet-of-Things (IoT) and computer architecture.
